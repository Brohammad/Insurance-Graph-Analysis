COMPREHENSIVE HEALTH INSURANCE POLICY
Policy Number: POL001
Effective Date: January 1, 2025

==================================================
COVERAGE SUMMARY
==================================================

This Comprehensive Health Insurance Policy provides extensive coverage for medical expenses, 
treatments, and healthcare services. The policy is designed to protect policyholders from 
high medical costs and ensure access to quality healthcare.

ANNUAL COVERAGE LIMIT: ₹10,00,000 (Ten Lakhs)
DEDUCTIBLE: ₹25,000 per policy year
CO-PAYMENT: 10% on all claims

==================================================
COVERED CONDITIONS AND TREATMENTS
==================================================

1. DIABETES MELLITUS
   - Coverage: Fully covered after 2-year waiting period
   - Includes: Insulin, oral medications, blood glucose monitoring supplies
   - Hospital care: ICU, general ward, specialist consultations
   - Complications: Diabetic neuropathy, retinopathy, nephropathy
   - Annual limit: Up to ₹2,00,000 for diabetes-related expenses

2. CARDIOVASCULAR DISEASES
   - Heart attacks and coronary artery disease: Fully covered
   - Angioplasty and stent procedures: Covered up to ₹3,50,000
   - Bypass surgery: Covered up to ₹5,00,000
   - Cardiac rehabilitation: Covered for 3 months post-surgery
   - Medications: ACE inhibitors, beta-blockers, anticoagulants

3. CANCER TREATMENT
   - Diagnosis: All diagnostic tests covered
   - Chemotherapy: Fully covered, no sub-limits
   - Radiation therapy: Fully covered
   - Immunotherapy: Covered up to ₹8,00,000 per year
   - Targeted therapy: Covered with pre-authorization
   - Bone marrow transplant: Covered up to policy limit

4. RESPIRATORY CONDITIONS
   - Asthma: Fully covered including medications and inhalers
   - COPD: Covered including oxygen therapy
   - Pneumonia: Hospitalization and treatment covered
   - Tuberculosis: Fully covered including extended treatment

5. ORTHOPEDIC PROCEDURES
   - Joint replacement: Hip, knee covered up to ₹4,00,000
   - Fracture treatment: Fully covered
   - Spinal surgery: Covered up to ₹5,00,000
   - Arthroscopy: Fully covered
   - Physiotherapy: 20 sessions per year covered

6. MATERNITY BENEFITS
   - Normal delivery: ₹50,000
   - Cesarean section: ₹75,000
   - Pre-natal care: 9 check-ups covered
   - Post-natal care: 6-week follow-up covered
   - Newborn coverage: Automatic from day 1

==================================================
EXCLUSIONS
==================================================

The following are NOT covered under this policy:

1. Cosmetic or aesthetic procedures
2. Experimental or investigational treatments
3. Self-inflicted injuries
4. Substance abuse treatment (unless rider purchased)
5. Pre-existing conditions in first 2 years (except with declaration)
6. Alternative medicine (Ayurveda, Homeopathy) - unless rider added
7. Obesity treatment and weight loss surgery
8. Dental procedures (except accident-related)
9. Vision correction surgery (LASIK)
10. Hearing aids and cochlear implants

==================================================
CLAIM PROCESS
==================================================

CASHLESS CLAIMS:
1. Present policy card at network hospital
2. Hospital verifies coverage and pre-authorizes treatment
3. Treatment provided without upfront payment
4. Hospital bills insurance company directly
5. Patient pays only deductible and co-payment

REIMBURSEMENT CLAIMS:
1. Pay hospital bills at non-network facility
2. Collect all bills, prescriptions, and discharge summary
3. Submit claim form within 30 days
4. Provide bank details for reimbursement
5. Claim processed within 15 working days

Required documents:
- Claim form (signed)
- Hospital bills and receipts
- Discharge summary
- Diagnostic test reports
- Prescriptions
- Photo ID proof
- Bank account details

==================================================
PREMIUM AND RENEWAL
==================================================

Annual Premium: Based on age, sum insured, and zone
Premium increases: 5-10% annually based on claims experience
No-claim bonus: 10% premium discount for claim-free years (cumulative up to 50%)
Grace period: 30 days for premium payment
Policy lapses if premium not paid within grace period

RENEWAL BENEFITS:
- Guaranteed renewal up to age 65
- No medical examination required for renewal
- Waiting period does not restart on renewal
- Accumulated no-claim bonus retained

==================================================
SPECIAL CONDITIONS
==================================================

PRE-EXISTING DISEASES:
Must be declared at policy inception. Coverage begins after 2-year waiting period.
Medication for pre-existing conditions covered after waiting period.

EMERGENCY CARE:
Accident and emergency care covered from day 1, even at non-network hospitals.
Ambulance charges covered up to ₹2,000 per hospitalization.

COVID-19 COVERAGE:
Hospitalization, ICU, ventilator support fully covered.
Home quarantine treatment covered up to ₹25,000.
Post-COVID complications covered for 90 days after discharge.
